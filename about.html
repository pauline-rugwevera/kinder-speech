About us

Here at Kinder Speech, our mission is to meet the needs of children in need of speech, language,articulation,and communication skills. We are committed to provide you with the best speech and language skills in a professional and dignified manner.

Kinder Speech is nestled along Ballymun road in Dublin, It was founded in 2016 .With over 5 years of experience in providing professional speech and language skills to the children around our community, Kinder Speech was founded to meet the needs of children in need of speech, language,articulation,and communication skills.

Drop by our offices or send us an email for any questions you might have. We are glad to answer them.
